THE CONCEPT

What is SubEasy?

SubEasy is a secure, intelligent dashboard engineered to organize your entire subscription ecosystem. It acts as a mission control for your digital life, unifying scattered services into one high-fidelity interface.

THE PURPOSE

The Subscription Trap.

Over $20 billion is wasted on unused subscriptions every year globally. Yet we still rely on memory and messy spreadsheets to track our own money. You sign up, enter your card, and forget... and pay.

Stop Paying for Nothing

No More Surprises

See Your Spending

Right now, you're relying on one fragile thing: Your Memory. When you forget a trial, you end up paying for it—not because you wanted to, but by accident.
